This is the classes and functions reference in surfify. Please refer to the gallery for further details, as the class and function raw specifications may not be enough to give full guidelines on their uses.

surfify

Helper module for surface integration in PyTorch.

Surface augmentations.

surfify.datasets

Utilities to load datasets, including methods to load and fetch popular reference datasets. It also features some artificial data generators.
